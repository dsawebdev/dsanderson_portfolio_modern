Bio

Hello World! My name is Devon Anderson and I am half-coding bootcamp/half self-taught Front End Web Developer and UX UI Designer. My passion for design and coding has recently taken back seat to another career path that although I love, it's not a true passion. It's time for me to get back to that feeling of pure creativity and regain my passion!

The conscious effort of this portfolio site will for you (the reader, supporter and potential cleint) to follow along in my journey back to design, coding and whatever else I feel thats next for me!

As I dig deep into brushing off the dust of things I have learned in the past, I am also challenged to add new technologies that will make me a more modern developer. In the past I have done remote work for a few agencies, been part of some small startups, managed SEO, and designed and created digital products for the small business owners in my area. My main goal for any cleint is to invoke a great user interface in my designs and gain an emotional user experience in the process. So lets get to it!!
