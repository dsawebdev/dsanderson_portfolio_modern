Bio

As I tend to the wounds of the burn marks of a non-creative corporate career. I feel I can no longer live a double life. My passion for design and coding has taken the back seat to a career that I enjoy, but don't love. It's time for me to get back to that feeling.

The conscious effort of this portfolio site will be to let you follow along in my journey, back to my path of pure creativity. I've done remote work for few agencies, been part of startups, and designed and created digital products for the small business owners in my area. I seek to invoke expressionism in my designs and gain an emotional experience rather than the norm and inmpression of what everyone else is doing.
